Our Vision

The WOMEN OF GRACE Organization was created to empower women and create opportunities for refugees and immigrants through education, integration, and rehabilitation of impoverished communities.

Our Mission

As a faith-based organization, we believe that giving has the power to change lives. Let’s use this power to make a positive difference by empowering women and providing a better life for those in need.
